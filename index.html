Our Story

Born in the heart of the rural countryside, Luminheart began as a personal journey of healing and a creative pastime following an accident. What started in a quiet moment has grown into an intimate passion for perfection.

We believe in delivering only the products we would choose for our own home—hand-poured with heart and crafted to be a true luxury. Our mission is simple: to offer premium, soul-warming scents and artisan quality, while remaining a thoughtful, homely luxury for everyone.
